The NADI Experience

Immerse yourself in the architectural symphony of NADI. From the vibrant energy of the Kilauan Digital Museum to the restorative serenity of our Geriatric Atriums, witness how spatial logic meets deep human empathy.

Geospatial Prominence

Strategic Connectivity

Commanding a highly strategic position at 3°53'59.85"N 102°22'39.00"E, the site boasts direct, unhindered accessibility via Route 64. Its immediate adjacency to the proposed KTM rail network facilitates unparalleled Transit-Oriented Development (TOD), effortlessly bridging urban connectivity with rural exclusivity.

The Structural Gap

Demographic Imperative

The district faces a severe housing deficit of 7,876 units amid strong owner-occupier demand. Concurrently, Jerantut is transitioning into an aging society, with the 65+ demographic exceeding the 7% threshold. NADI's tripartite mission is a direct architectural response: to foster a resilient retirement ecosystem, capture the global wellness market, and staunch youth outmigration via high-yield technical employment and bespoke housing provisions.

Spatial Symphony

The Masterplan

A breathtaking 1,110.64-acre canvas, masterfully zoned to ensure 49% of the native topography remains entirely untouched. This commitment to conservation preserves the delicate ecological integrity that defines the heart of Pahang.

Circulation & Mobility

Abandoning the rigid, monotonous grids typical of conventional estates, our organic layout mimics natural river swales. Curvilinear secondary roads naturally slow vehicular traffic to below 30km/h, prioritizing pedestrian safety and paving the way for our magnificent 1.5km green Atrium Walk spine.

  • ■ Main Arteries: 100' high-efficiency traffic corridors.
  • ■ Secondary Networks: 60' avenues integrated with traffic calming elements.
  • ■ Pedestrian First: Safe, walkable biophilic neighborhoods promoting active aging.
Universal Design Paradigm

Symbiotic Interflow

The NADI Interflow Framework is a radical, empathetic reimagining of residential zoning. Rather than isolating demographics, we purposefully weave them together. Ground-level, barrier-free bungalows embrace our elderly residents with absolute dignity, while flexible upper-level studio configurations cater directly to dynamic, remote-working youths. This intentional design fosters a multi-generational symbiosis rarely achieved in modern development.

Smart Healthcare Integration

Every residence is future-proofed with NADI Smart Sensor Arrays for preventative ambient monitoring, built-in Caregiver Alert Stations, and direct telemetry linked instantly to the central Geriatric Hospital.

A Masterful Orchestration of Growth

Implementation Strategy

To strictly mitigate risk and ensure sustainable market absorption, NADI employs a calculated "Transit-First" phasing logic. This triggers immediate economic momentum before expanding into specialized, high-yield healthcare sectors.

Degupan NADI

The Youth & Transit Catalyst. We commence by activating Transit-Oriented Development (TOD) immediately adjacent to the KTM station. By prioritizing NADI University and agile startup spaces, we construct a foundational workforce pipeline and secure vital regulatory momentum.

Bayu NADI

The Commerce & Tourism Expansion. With the workforce established, Phase 2 erects the MICE Center, premium hotels, and the heavy bio-medical manufacturing hubs. This aggressively drives regional visitor engagement and foreign direct investment.

Hayat NADI

The Pinnacle of Wellness. The final maturation of the township. Delivering the specialized Geriatric Hospital and ultra-premium Eco-Nadi sanctuaries. The ecosystem is now fully capable of supporting a globally recognized retirement destination.

Macro & Micro Risk Mitigation

Site & PESTLE Analysis

Rigorous evaluation of environmental sensitivities (272.19 acres of watershed basin, 258.71 acres of river buffers) juxtaposed with exhaustive Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Legal, and Environmental (PESTLE) due diligence.
